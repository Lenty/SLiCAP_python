Detector referred noise spectrum

The spectral density of the total output noise can be written as:

\begin{equation} S_{out}=R_{s}^{2} S_{i} + 4 R_{s} T k + S_{v}\,\left[ \mathrm{\frac{V^{2}}{Hz}}\right] \end{equation}

Noise figure

The noise figure is obtained as:

\begin{equation} F=\frac{10 \log{\left(\frac{R_{s} S_{i}}{4 T k} + 1 + \frac{S_{v}}{4 R_{s} T k} \right)}}{\log{\left(10 \right)}}\,\left[ \mathrm{dB}\right] \end{equation}
